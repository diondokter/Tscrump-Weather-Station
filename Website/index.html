About

Make Weather Stations Great Again! is a school project made by the Tscrump Group. Made out of basic components such as a Temperature and Air Pressure Sensor, A Brightness Sensor, Humidity Sensor and a large scale Data Network.

Wether you are a granny or a little kid, everyone likes this wether station. Control it at home or do it while being abroad. You can check the wether in your home town using the LORA network.
